Event Overview

The Jakarta Premiere Night is the crown jewel of the city's cinematic calendar, a prestigious evening dedicated to showcasing the absolute best of local and international visionaries.

This year, we transform the iconic Taman Ismail Marzuki into a sanctuary of storytelling and technical brilliance.

Expect an immersive atmosphere where the pulse of Jakarta’s urban energy meets the refined artistry of world-class filmmaking.
